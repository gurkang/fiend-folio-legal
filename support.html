Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete a playthrough?

Go to the Stats tab, tap on the playthrough you want to delete, and select "Delete Playthrough." This action cannot be undone.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not sync data to the cloud. We recommend backing up your device regularly if you want to preserve your progress.

Can I transfer my progress to a new device?

Currently, data is stored locally and cannot be transferred between devices. This feature may be added in a future update.
